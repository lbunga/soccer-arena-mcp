## 1. Cancellation Policy

Bookings may be cancelled in writing (email to the address on file or by replying
to the calendar invite). The refund schedule is calculated from the **scheduled
start time** of the session:

- **More than 48 hours before start:** full refund (100% of the slot fee), no service charge.
- **Between 24 and 48 hours before start:** 50% refund.
- **Less than 24 hours before start:** no refund; the slot fee is forfeited.
- **No-show / arrival after the no-show threshold (see §4):** treated as <24h cancellation; no refund.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days. Refunds
are never issued in cash. Booking codes for cancelled sessions are deactivated
and can no longer unlock the web app mini-game.

## 2. Rebooking Policy

A confirmed booking may be moved to a different open slot (rebooked) without
financial penalty if the rebooking request is submitted **more than 24 hours
before** the original scheduled start. Within 24 hours, a rebooking is treated as
a cancellation followed by a new booking and is subject to the §1 refund
schedule for the original slot.

Each booking may be rebooked at most **twice**. Further changes require
cancelling and creating a new booking. The new slot must be available at the
time the rebooking is processed; the system does not hold slots.

## 3. Late Arrival Policy

Sessions begin at the booked start time. Arena staff will release the field at
the booked start time regardless of who has arrived. Groups arriving late lose
the unused minutes — the session **does not** extend past the scheduled end
time, because the next group has the slot afterward.

A grace period of **15 minutes** applies for groups that have checked in at the
front desk. Groups still outside the building 15 minutes after the start time
are considered no-shows (see §4).

## 4. No-Show Policy

A booking is recorded as a no-show if no member of the party has checked in at
the front desk within **15 minutes** of the scheduled start time. No-shows
forfeit the full slot fee (no refund), and the slot is released for walk-in use
at the discretion of arena staff.

Three no-shows within a rolling 12-month period result in a 30-day suspension
of the email address from making new bookings.

## 5. Refund Policy

Refunds are governed by §1 (cancellation) and §6 (weather/closure). Refunds for
disputes outside those categories (e.g. equipment failure that materially
shortened a session) are reviewed case-by-case; submit a written request within
**7 days** of the affected session. Approved refunds are issued to the original
payment method within 5 business days.

## 6. Weather & Facility Closure Policy

VirtualSoccerArena is an **indoor** facility; rain, snow, or extreme
temperatures outside the building do not by themselves cause closures. The
arena will close (and refund affected sessions in full) only when:

- A municipal emergency order requires closure;
- A facility issue (power, HVAC, water, structural) makes the field unsafe;
- A statutory holiday applies (see §16).

In all closure cases the booking holder is contacted by email and offered either
(a) a full refund or (b) a rebook with the rebooking-fee waiver applied.

## 7. Code of Conduct

All participants agree to a respectful, sporting environment. The following are
grounds for immediate removal without refund:

- Verbal or physical abuse of staff, opponents, or referees;
- Discriminatory language or behaviour;
- Damage to the playing surface, nets, or equipment;
- Use of metal studs (only moulded studs or turf shoes permitted; see §13);
- Bringing alcohol, cannabis, or other intoxicants on premises.

Repeat violations may result in a permanent ban from the facility.

## 8. Equipment Rental

Game balls, bibs (pinnies), and pump-up time are included in every booking at
no extra cost. Additional rentals available at the front desk:

- **Goalkeeper gloves:** $5 / session
- **Shin guards (loaner):** $3 / session
- **Match-quality ball (FIFA Pro):** $10 / session
- **Cones (set of 12):** $5 / session
- **GoPro mount on goal frame:** $15 / session (bring your own camera)

A valid government-issued ID is held as deposit until rented items are returned.

## 9. Group & Team Bookings

Groups of 10+ players booking the same slot may apply a **10% group discount**
at checkout. Recurring weekly team bookings (8 or more weeks consecutive) apply
a **15% team-season discount** and lock the slot for the duration; cancellation
of a team-season booking requires 14 days notice and forfeits one week's slot
fee.

## 10. Membership Tiers

Memberships are optional; pay-per-session is always available.

- **Open** (no membership): standard rates apply, see §15.
- **Player ($29 / month):** 10% off off-peak, 5% off peak; free shin guard rental.
- **Pro ($59 / month):** 15% off all rates; free goalkeeper gloves; one free guest pass per month.
- **Squad ($149 / month, up to 6 named players):** 20% off all rates, priority on rebooking, dedicated locker.

Memberships are billed monthly and may be cancelled at any time effective the
next billing cycle.

## 11. Payment Policy

Accepted payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, Interac debit, and Apple/Google
Pay. **Cash is not accepted** at this location. All prices are quoted in
Canadian Dollars (CAD) and include HST where applicable. Payment is required at
the time of booking; bookings without successful payment are not confirmed and
do not hold a slot.

## 12. Liability Waiver

By entering the playing area, participants acknowledge that indoor soccer
involves inherent risk of injury. VirtualSoccerArena, its staff, and its
landlords are not liable for injuries sustained during ordinary play.
Participants under the age of 18 must have a waiver signed by a parent or legal
guardian on file. Waivers are valid for 12 months and may be renewed online.

## 13. Footwear & Apparel

Permitted: turf shoes, indoor soccer shoes, moulded studs (FG/MG), trainers
with non-marking soles. **Not permitted:** metal studs, bare feet, sandals,
street shoes that mark the turf. Goalkeepers may wear padded apparel; players
are encouraged to wear shin guards (loaners available, see §8).

## 14. Age Rules

- **Under 6:** must be in a supervised parent-and-child session; general bookings not permitted.
- **6–12:** must be accompanied by an adult on the field at all times.
- **13–17:** may book independently with a guardian-signed waiver on file.
- **18+:** standard policy applies.

## 15. Facility Rates (Reference)

Rates are charged per 2-hour session and include the field, balls, bibs, and
basic supervision. Memberships (§10) and group/team discounts (§9) reduce the
displayed rate.

| | Off-peak | Peak |
|---|---|---|
| Weekday | $120 | $160 |
| Weekend | $150 | $200 |

- **Weekday peak:** 16:00 and 18:00 session starts.
- **Weekend peak:** 10:00, 12:00, 14:00, 16:00 session starts.

## 16. Holiday Closures (2026 Demo Window)

The facility is closed all day on the following statutory holidays during the
demo window:

- Wednesday, July 1 — Canada Day
- Monday, August 3 — August Civic Holiday
- Monday, September 7 — Labour Day
- Monday, October 12 — Thanksgiving
- Wednesday, November 11 — Remembrance Day
- Friday, December 25 — Christmas Day
- Saturday, December 26 — Boxing Day

Sessions scheduled on these dates are not bookable; the system refuses
availability requests for these dates.

## 17. Privacy & Data Policy

VirtualSoccerArena collects only the information needed to operate bookings:
name, email, and session history. Information is stored in a local database and
is not sold, shared, or used for marketing. Booking codes are not personally
identifying and may be shared at the booker's discretion. Account data is
deleted on request within 30 days.

## 18. Lost & Found

Items left at the facility are kept at the front desk for **30 days**. Valuable
items (electronics, jewellery, IDs) are logged and stored in a secured cabinet.
After 30 days unclaimed items are donated. The arena is not responsible for
items left on the premises.

## 19. Accessibility

The facility is wheelchair-accessible. Adapted equipment (powered wheelchair
bumpers, lightweight balls, audio whistles) is available on request at no extra
charge. Service animals are welcome on the deck but not on the playing surface.

## 20. Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I bring spectators?** Yes — the gallery seats up to 40 and is free for
spectators.

**Can I pay at the door?** No — bookings require online payment (see §11).

**Is parking included?** Free parking is available on-site for 90 minutes per
session.

**Do you sell drinks/snacks?** A vending machine is on-site. No outside
alcohol or cannabis is permitted (§7).

**Can I rebook the same slot every week?** Yes — see §9 for the team-season
option.

**What happens if I lose my booking code?** Email support; we will look it up
by name and email.

**Do membership discounts stack with group discounts?** No — only the higher of
the two applies.

**Can children watch from the field?** Children under 6 in a supervised
parent-and-child session may be on the field; otherwise minors not playing must
remain in the gallery.
